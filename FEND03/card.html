Pangolin

Pangolins are armored anteaters that can walk on two legs.

  • Name Meaning: "thing that rolls up"
  • Habitat: Tropical Asia and Africa
  • Diet: Insects
  • Size: 12-40"

Although it looks ready for battle in its coat of armor, the pangolin is solitary and just eats insects. Some species of pangolin have prehensile tails that they use to climb trees and empty out insect nests.
